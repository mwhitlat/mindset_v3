1. **Overall Health Score** (0-10)
2. **Content Balance Score** (0-10)
3. **Source Diversity Score** (0-10)
4. **Time Management Score** (0-10)
5. **Credibility Score** (0-10)
6. **Content Tone Score** (0-10)
7. **Political Bias Score** (0-10)

### Detailed Breakdowns
1. **Content Categories**: News, Entertainment, Professional, Educational
2. **Time Analysis**: Daily patterns, peak hours, session lengths
3. **Source Analysis**: Domain diversity, credibility ratings
4. **Behavioral Patterns**: Navigation habits, engagement depth
5. **Trend Analysis**: Week-over-week changes
6. **Tone Analysis**: Cynical vs. uplifting content distribution
7. **Political Analysis**: Liberal, conservative, centrist source balance

### Insights & Alerts
1. **Pattern Recognition**: Behavioral insights
2. **Bias Detection**: Echo chamber warnings
3. **Tone Awareness**: Cynical vs. uplifting content alerts
4. **Political Balance**: Ideological diversity monitoring
5. **Goal Tracking**: Progress toward targets
6. **Recommendations**: Personalized suggestions
7. **Achievements**: Positive reinforcement

This user experience focuses on **clarity**, **actionability**, and **privacy**, while providing meaningful insights that help users understand and improve their digital consumption patterns.
