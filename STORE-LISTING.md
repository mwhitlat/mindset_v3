# Mindset - Digital Diet Tracker

## Short Description
Privacy-first browser extension that helps you understand your digital content consumption patterns and their impact on your mindset.

## Long Description
Mindset is a privacy-first Chrome extension that empowers you to reflect on your digital diet. It analyzes the content you consume—news, social, entertainment, and more—entirely on your device, never sending your data anywhere. Mindset helps you:

- See the political and emotional bias of every page you visit
- Track your long-term content diet: liberal/conservative, uplifting/cynical, and more
- Visualize your digital habits with a beautiful dashboard
- Get real-time feedback and smart notifications
- Stay in control: all data is local, with optional encryption
- Export or delete your data at any time

## Features
- Real-time content analysis (political bias, tone, credibility)
- Weekly and daily summaries of your digital diet
- Visual status bar and dashboard
- Smart notifications and engagement hooks
- 100% local processing and storage
- Optional AES-256 encryption
- Minimal permissions, strict security
