# Privacy Policy for Mindset - Digital Diet Tracker

**Effective Date:** [Today]

## Overview
Mindset is a privacy-first browser extension that helps you understand your digital content consumption patterns. We believe your data belongs to you. All analysis and storage happens locally on your device.

## What Data Is Collected?
- **Browsing Activity:** The extension analyzes the content and metadata of web pages you visit (title, URL, text, domain).
- **Content Analysis:** Mindset calculates scores for content tone, political bias, credibility, and other metrics.
- **Settings & Preferences:** Your extension settings and preferences are stored locally.

## How Is Data Used?
- **Local Analysis Only:** All data is processed and stored locally in your browser. No data is sent to external servers or third parties.
- **User Control:** You can export, clear, or delete your data at any time from the extension settings.
- **No Tracking:** Mindset does not track you across devices or sessions. No personal identifiers are collected.

## Data Sharing
- **No External Transmission:** Mindset does not transmit, sell, or share your data with anyone.
- **No Third-Party Analytics:** We do not use Google Analytics or any other analytics/tracking services.

## Security
- **Encryption:** You may optionally enable local encryption for your data (AES-256-GCM).
- **Permissions:** The extension requests only the minimum permissions required for functionality.
- **Content Security Policy:** Mindset enforces a strict CSP to prevent XSS and data leaks.

## User Rights
- **Access:** You can view all data Mindset stores about your browsing.
- **Delete:** You can delete all data at any time.
- **Export:** You can export your data for personal use or review.

## Contact
For questions or concerns, contact the developer at mindset.extension.beta@gmail.com.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy to reflect changes in the extension or legal requirements. Updates will be posted here and in the Chrome Web Store listing.
