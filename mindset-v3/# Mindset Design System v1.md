# Mindset Design System v1.0

---

## 1. Principles

* Calm over clever
* Clarity over decoration
* Consistency over experimentation
* Negative space over density
* Neutral tone over emotional tone

---

## 2. Layout

Base grid: 8px
Section spacing: 24px
Internal spacing: 16px
Tight grouping: 8px

Max width: 520px
Centered layout.

---

## 3. Typography

Font stack:
-apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", Roboto, sans-serif

Scale:

* Display: 20–22px
* Body: 14–15px
* Secondary: 12–13px
* Label: 11–12px

No italics.
Minimal bold.
No decorative typography.

---

## 4. Colors

Background: #FFFFFF
Primary text: #111111
Secondary text: #666666
Divider: #EAEAEA

Accent: Single muted blue (e.g., #4A6CF7)

No emotional colors (red/green signaling).

---

## 5. Components

Allowed:

* Section container (border only)
* Divider
* Simple bar chart
* Horizontal distribution chart
* Text block
* Minimal toggle

Not allowed:

* Drop shadows
* Gradients
* Animated backgrounds
* Gamification
* Badges
* Decorative elements

---

## 6. Data Visualization

* Max 3 colors
* No ranking by ideology
* No moral labels
* No intensity highlighting
* Flat, static charts

---

## 7. Density Constraint

If adding content:

* Remove equivalent content.
* Avoid scroll overload.
* Prefer fewer insights over clutter.

---

## 8. Change Control

New:

* Font sizes
* Colors
* Layout patterns
* Component types

Require explicit approval.

---

End of v1.0
