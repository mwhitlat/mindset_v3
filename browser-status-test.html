What are Browser Status Indicators?

Browser status indicators appear at the bottom of the browser window, similar to how browsers show security status, connection info, or other native browser information:

  • Native Integration - Looks and feels like part of the browser
  • Non-intrusive - Positioned at bottom, never blocks content
  • Comprehensive Info - Shows content type, credibility, and tone
  • Visual Feedback - Color-coded borders for quick credibility assessment
  • Always Visible - Consistent location across all sites

Look for the status bar at the bottom of this browser window!
